Privacy Policy		 JJTeach understands your concerns about how information about you is used and shared. We appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your personal information for our services on the JJTeach websites. By visiting JJTeach.com, you are expressly accepting the practices described in this Privacy Notice.

Information gathered by JJTeach

To create a personalised training portal and upload content, create, view and  share presentations, you need a JJTeach Account. Your JJTeach account is protected using credentials supplied during account sign-up process. We receive and store all information provided by you on JJTeach
When you use JJTeach, we record all the information about your interaction with our sites, services, content, quizzes, polls, retries, statistics on page views, which videos you watch, which other users you communicate with, the frequency and size of data transfers, information you display about yourself as well as information you click on in JJTeach (including UI elements, settings), computer and connection information, IP address and standard web log information. This information is collected, and reported to your channel administrators & authors to improve your viewing experience, increase ROI and business results

Use of Cookies

JJTeach creates and uses cookies to:

  • To provide ease of use and allow you to login without having to re-enter your information during your visit or the next visit;
  • For research purposes like total number of visitors, pages viewed, monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns etc.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies, if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to use all the features and services of JJTeach that depend on cookies. If you choose to accept cookies, you also have the ability to later delete cookies that you have accepted.

You agree that we may use your personal information to:

Except as described in this statement, we will not disclose your personal information outside of JJTeach and its affiliates without your consent.

We store your content on our servers and treat your information as an asset and use various technological means available to us to protect all information.

Account Protection

Your password is the key to your personalised training platform. Use unique numbers, letters and special characters, and do not disclose your password. If you do share your password or your personal information with others, remember that you are responsible for all actions taken in the name of your account. If you lose control of your password, you may lose substantial control over your personal information or content and may be subject to legally binding actions taken on your behalf. Therefore, if your credentials have been compromised for any reason, you should immediately notify JJTeach and change your credentials.

No Spam, Spyware or Spoofing
